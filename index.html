🧪 Labtech Department: Cascading tier bonuses (Tier 1 → Tier 2 → Tier 3 → Base). Edit rates in Config tab.

Formats: "ID - 3 cap 1 deli" or "ID - Captchas Completed: 10 Deliveries Made : 5"


    


    
    

      

        🏥 Medical Department: Pillbox Hill (PH1/PH2) and Sandy Shores (SH) with Day/Night/Late-Night shifts. Can do lobby or on-calls.
      

      

        
        
Examples: "ID - 8H PH2 Night", "ID - 6H On Calls Day", "ID - 2H SH Late Night"

        
      

      

        
        
      

      

    


    
    

      

        🚑 Ambulance Service: On-call hours and lobby shifts. Can do any lobby or on-calls.
      

      

        
        
Examples: "ID - 2H On Calls Day", "ID - 1H PH1 Night", "ID - 3H SH Day"

        
      

      

        
        
      

      

    


    
    

      

        🩺 Medical Students: Same rates as Medical Department (PH1/PH2/SH + On Calls). Can do lobby or on-calls but NOT FTO/Standby/RO.
      

      

        
        
Examples: "ID - 2H PH Day + 6H On Calls Day", "ID - 8H PH2 Night"

        
      

      

        
        
      

      

    


    
    

      

        🚁 Rescue Operations: Scripted and Unscripted operations. Can also do ANY lobby (PH1/PH2/SH) or on-calls.
      

      

        
        
Examples: "ID - 1 Scripted 5 Unscripted" or "ID - 8H PH1 Day + 2 Scripted" or "ID - 5H On Calls Night"

        
      

      

        
        
      

      

    


    
    

      

        🥼 Field Training Officer: Training bonuses for Day 1, Day 2, Labtech, and Refresher sessions. FTO cannot do lobby/on-calls/standby.
      

      

        
        
Format: "ID - 4 Day 1 6 Day 2 2 Labtech" or "ID - 5 Day 1"

        
      

      

        
        
      

      

    


    
    

      

        👑 High Command: Same as Medical/Ambulance + Standby shifts. Can do ANY lobby (PH1/PH2/SH), on-calls, or standby.
      

      

        
        
Examples: "ID - 28H PH1 Day" or "ID - 8H PH2 Night + 2H Standby" or "ID - 5H On Calls Day"
